Tourism Categories


Adventure Tours

Whether it’s hiking in the Knuckles Mountain Range, white water rafting in Kitulgala, or diving in the coral reefs, Sri Lanka offers endless adventures for thrill-seekers.

Historical Places

Visit ancient ruins, castles, and landmarks with rich cultural history.

Beaches & Islands

With its golden sandy beaches, clear waters, and palm-lined coasts, Sri Lanka is a tropical haven. Popular beach destinations include Mirissa, Bentota, and the Unawatuna Bay.

Wildlife & Nature

From lush rainforests to vast national parks, Sri Lanka is a paradise for nature lovers. The country is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including elephants, leopards, and rare bird species.

Cultural Heritage

Sri Lanka boasts a rich history dating back thousands of years, home to eight UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the sacred city of Kandy, the ancient city of Anuradhapura, and the Sigiriya Rock Fortress.

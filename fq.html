F&Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to book a tour?

You can book a tour by visiting our website and filling out the booking form. Alternatively, you can contact us directly through phone or email.

What is the cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel your tour, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the tour date will incur a 50% cancellation fee.

What is the payment method?

We accept credit card payments, bank transfers, and cash payments. Please note that a 2% surcharge applies to credit card transactions.

What is the dress code for the tour?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for walking. Please avoid wearing high heels or open-toed shoes.

Can I bring my children on the tour?

Yes, children are welcome on our tours. However, please note that children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

What if I have a medical condition?

Please inform us of any medical conditions or special requirements at the time of booking. We will do our best to accommodate your needs.
